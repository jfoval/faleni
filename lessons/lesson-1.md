# Faleni · Lesson 1 — Sounds: hear & say

> **Note for readers on GitHub:** this lesson is *interactive* (tap-to-hear audio
> for all 16 sounds), so it isn't authored as plain markdown like the others —
> it's generated by `build_sounds()` in [`site/build.py`](../site/build.py).
>
> **Read/hear it on the live site → <https://jfoval.github.io/faleni/lesson-1.html>**

It covers, by ear, before any vocabulary:

- the **three traps** for English speakers — `j` = "y" (not the English "j"),
  pure vowels (no gliding), and stress on the first syllable;
- the **5 vowels** (`a e i o u`) and **11 consonants** (`p t k f s h m n l w j`),
  each with its IPA, an English keyword, and a play button;
- whole phrases to repeat.
